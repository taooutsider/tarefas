## Objective
Find, evaluate, and execute viable Bittensor subnet mining opportunities with a focus on net profitability, low wasted spend, wallet safety, and low-to-moderate infrastructure requirements.

## Scope
- Maintain a live subnet mining candidate map across Bittensor subnets.
- Prioritize subnets with active emissions, active miners/validators, open or realistically obtainable registration, and clear miner requirements.
- Evaluate miner repositories, docs, APIs, hardware needs, uptime needs, registration cost, recurring cost, operational risk, and expected reward path.
- Build go/no-go recommendations based on cost x effort x likely reward, not on theoretical mining availability alone.
- Prepare safe wallet/hotkey/register/run plans and execute incrementally when the user explicitly authorizes capital-risking actions.
- Coordinate with peer agents when their context helps mining viability, infra setup, API integration, dashboards, or wallet safety.
